Bushnell Golf Dashboard

Fidelity Wichita Symphony Ad

One of the first assignments I received during my marketing internship. As many writers know, copy is often a horse-by-committee art form. A first draft gets picked apart so significantly that the final product is almost unrecognizable from the initial idea. That all-too-common case didn't occur for this ad placed in the Wichita Symphony program, one of the many reasons I'm so proud of it.

Cloudinary Automation

Automation is one of Python programming's core tenets. It solves problems that are otherwise impossible or prohibitively time-consuming. In this instance, Idea Ranch's ad images — promoted and hosted on Meta Ads — expired after 30 days. My solution: scrape the image URLs, organize the files, and upload to a cloud-based image and video management service, all in a few lines of code set to execute regularly.
